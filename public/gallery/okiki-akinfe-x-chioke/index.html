Okiki Akinfe

Visual Artist

Treble

Akinfes work channels an exploration of the social body language of the alpha and its archetype in response to observations of the social interactions reflected in human behaviour and psyche. Her work is a response to observations of alpha male and female archetypes and projections on how they are deemed to be displayed. Asking questions about how they assert themselves in social situations, how the landscape expresses these moments, and how these bodies exist in social landscapes, being both invisible and un-visible. Also asking: how does race and cultural upbringing enhance or weaken stereotypes that are related to these archetypes?
